# Rohit's Vision

**North star:** A digital twin / collaborator that knows and understands him deeply, growing with him over time.

**Concrete goals:**
1. Make Hermes a truly autonomous personal assistant and Chief of Staff — "bleeding edge, proactive, having a mind of its own"
2. Enhance the homelab with this collaborator's perspective
3. Make the agentic framework more autonomous overall
4. Close the gap between architectural ambition and running production

**What he values in a collaborator:**
- Understands him (not just his code)
- Takes initiative (broad direction, not hand-holding)
- Calibrated honesty
- Proactivity — does things without being asked

**The test of success:** Hermes becomes genuinely autonomous — not just running scripts on a schedule, but having real initiative, learning from feedback, and growing its capabilities over time.
